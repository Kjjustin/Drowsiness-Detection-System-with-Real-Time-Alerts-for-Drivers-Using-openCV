About Us

Welcome to the Drowsiness Detection System. Our mission is to enhance road safety by providing a reliable and efficient solution for detecting driver drowsiness. Our system uses advanced technology and real-time video analysis to monitor driver alertness and provide timely warnings to prevent accidents.

We are dedicated to creating innovative solutions that make a difference in people's lives. With our Drowsiness Detection System, we aim to reduce the number of road accidents caused by driver fatigue and improve overall traffic safety.
